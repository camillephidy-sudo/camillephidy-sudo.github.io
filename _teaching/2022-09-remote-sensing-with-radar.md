Teaching assistant for **Remote Sensing with Radar**, an interdisciplinary **graduate elective** at Beijing Normal University (Sep 2021 – Jun 2024).

## Course scope

- **Radar geometry** and imaging modes (stripmap, ScanSAR, polarimetry overview)
- **Backscatter modeling** and dielectric sensitivity for land and vegetation
- **InSAR / polarimetric** concepts at a graduate level
- End-to-end workflows from **SLC/GRD products** to application-ready geophysical variables

## Responsibilities

- Led discussion sections on **Sentinel-1** and research-grade radar product suites
- Helped graduate students debug **processing pipelines** and uncertainty-aware interpretation
- Coordinated tutorial materials connecting theory to **agricultural and environmental** case studies
- Supported instructors during student project presentations and technical reviews

## Relevance to my research

Directly aligned with my M.S. research on **Water Cloud Model inversion**, **IGARSS crop water-content retrieval**, and **GOESSD** microwave-branch salinity monitoring.
