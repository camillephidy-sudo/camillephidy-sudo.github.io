Teaching assistant for **Analyses and Applications of Microwave Remote Sensing Products**, an interdisciplinary **graduate elective** at Beijing Normal University (Sep 2021 – Jun 2024).

## Course scope

- Catalog and compare **operational microwave products** (soil moisture, cryosphere, ocean wind, vegetation optical depth proxies)
- **Quality assessment**, gap filling, and scale mismatch between swath observations and models
- Application modules for **hydrology, agriculture, and hazard monitoring**
- Best practices for coupling satellite products with **in-situ networks** and model assimilation

## Responsibilities

- Guided graduate projects on **product intercomparison** and error budgeting
- Taught workflow skills for **reproducible analysis** (scripting, metadata, versioned configs)
- Facilitated case studies linking classroom exercises to **regional monitoring** problems
- Supported peer review of student reports and capstone presentations

## Relevance to my research

Prepared students—and sharpened my own practice—for **operational GOESSD-style pipelines**, **YAML-driven MLOps**, and translating research prototypes into stakeholder-facing monitoring products.
